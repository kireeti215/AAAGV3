Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I join the AAAG Alumni Directory?▾

Visit the Alumni Directory page and click "Join Directory". Fill in your details and submit. The committee will review and approve your profile.

When is the Maata-Baata session held?▾

Maata-Baata sessions are held every Saturday from 7:00 PM to 8:00 PM online. Session links are shared in the AAAG WhatsApp group.

Who can join AAAG?▾

Any alumnus who studied at AP Residential School, Gandipalem — from the first batch of 1986 to the most recent — is welcome to join AAAG.

Is there a membership fee?▾

AAAG membership and the alumni directory are completely free. Participation in Maata-Baata sessions is also free.

How can I suggest a topic for Maata-Baata?▾

Use the contact form above and select "Suggestion / Feedback" as the reason, or visit Programs & Events page to suggest a topic.

How do I volunteer for AAAG activities?▾

Use the contact form above and select "Join as Volunteer". Describe your area of interest and the committee will connect you with the right wing.
