About AAAG

AAAG (Alumni Association of APRS Gandipalem) is a platform that brings together alumni to connect, collaborate, and contribute towards meaningful impact.

Our Vision

Where We Are Headed

To emerge as a strong and credible alumni association that serves as a long-term support system for the school, while fostering a meaningful and sustainable connection between the institution, its present students, and its alumni.

"Together, we grow.
Together, we give back." మన అనుభవం… మరొకరికి మార్గం.

— AAAG Vision Statement

Our Mission

What We Stand For

Every initiative we run, every program we design — flows from these five commitments.

1

To actively support the overall development of the school wherever required.

2

To guide present students with realistic, experience-based insights.

3

To support alumni with dignity, inclusion, and responsibility.

4

To function through defined systems and processes rather than individual dependence.

5

To promote transparency, cooperation, and accountability in all activities.

What Guides Us

Core Values

These five values are the foundation of every decision AAAG makes.

🤝

Respect

We respect teachers, students, alumni, and all stakeholders equally and without exception.

🌐

Collaboration

We believe in collective progress over individual authority — together, we achieve more.

🔍

Transparency

We maintain clarity and openness in all communication and decisions across the association.

✅

Responsibility

We take ownership of our commitments and ensure accountability in everything we do.

♾️

Continuity

We build systems that sustain and grow beyond any individual — for generations to come.

How It All Began

Our Story

🌱

January 20, 2020

A Spark from a Visit

2019లో school auditorium repair జరుగుతున్న సమయంలో, కొంతమంది alumni తమ school ని చూడటానికి వచ్చారు. ఆ visit లో ఒక spark పుట్టింది —

"మనం batch by batch కాదు, అందరం కలిసి school కి ఏదైనా చేయగలం కదా?"

ఆ ఒక్క conversation నుండి AAAG పుట్టింది. January 20, 2020 న officially launched అయింది — ఒక shared purpose తో, generations of alumni ని కలిపే ఒక platform గా.

Our Initiatives

What We Do

Four focused pillars through which AAAG delivers impact for alumni, students, and the school.

📋

Alumni Directory

Building a strong, searchable alumni network — connecting all batches from 1986 to today in one unified platform.

💬

Alumni Maata-Baata

A knowledge sharing and guidance platform where senior alumni share experiences and insights with juniors and students.

📅

Programs & Events

Organizing engagement programs, reunions, webinars, and community events to keep the APRS Gandipalem spirit alive.

🌱

Initiatives

Supporting school and student development activities — from scholarships to infrastructure, wherever alumni can contribute.
